Project Description

In Beat Kontrol, it allows me to use my drum kit to directly affect the aesthetics of my screen. By directly in putting my audio signals into the browser, I am able to use the analysed frequencies to control the visuals. This is done by mapping the frequcies into groups which then will be assigned to different actions. This would give me full control over the visuals that are displayed on the screen, as well as allowing me full musical control using my principal instrument, the drums. I would be able to produce a piece that I would have both musical and visual control over. Inversely, one would be able to control the aesthetics of the piece by pressing on the keyboard, or mouse.
